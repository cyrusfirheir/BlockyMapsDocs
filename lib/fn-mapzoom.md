`setup.bm.mapZoom([level])`

'Zooms' the camera in or out of the map to the specified level.

**Since:**
- `v1.0.0`

**Parameters:**
- `level` : (optional, *integer*) Percentage of the zoom. Recommended range from 10 to 250. Default is 100.

**Example:**
```js
// zooming in
setup.bm.mapZoom(150);

// zooming out
setup.bm.mapZoom(50);
```
